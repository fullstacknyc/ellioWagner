    WELCOME TO DEBWAGNER.COM

    Welcome to the online home of Deb Wagner a Professional Trumpet Player, WAG Records Owner (Grammy Nod), Music Director (OSCAR Governors Ball), and the award-winning Belle Air Brass. Ms. Wagner has left an indelible mark on the music & entertainment industry.

     

    Explore Deb's musical journey, from her early days as a budding musician to her awe-inspiring performances, and stay updated on her latest projects and collaborations. Ms. Wagner's dedication to her craft and her ability to transcend musical boundaries have earned her the admiration of audiences and fellow musicians alike.

    My new endeavor, PRESIDENT: INCLUSIVE ARTS EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION, brings the arts into the lives of students with disabilities.

    Thank you for joining us on this musical journey, stay tuned for exciting updates and announcements, and don't forget to connect with us on social media to be a part of Deb's ever-growing musical community.
